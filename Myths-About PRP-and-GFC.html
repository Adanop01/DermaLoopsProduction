Introduction

Introduction:

PRP and GFC have become buzzwords in the skincare and haircare world. But with popularity comes misinformation. Let’s bust the top myths holding people back from life-changing results.

Myth 1: “You need 10 sessions to see results”

Truth: Most patients see visible improvement in just 2–4 sessions if the treatment is done properly with good-quality tubes like Regenloop.

❌ Myth 2: “These treatments are only for men”

Truth: Women experience equal or even more noticeable results with PRP and GFC due to hormonal fluctuations and postpartum hair loss.

❌ Myth 3: “Once treated, hair never falls again”

Truth: Hair follicles age just like skin. Maintenance sessions every 6–8 months are recommended for long-term health.

✅ Reality:

PRP and GFC are safe, science-backed, and effective for both men and women when used correctly.

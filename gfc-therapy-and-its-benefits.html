What is GFC Therapy & Why It’s the Future of Hair Regrowth

Hair loss is one of the most common concerns faced by both men and women today. With lifestyle stress, hormonal imbalance, and genetics all playing a role, finding a safe and effective treatment has become essential. Enter *GFC Therapy (Growth Factor Concentrate)* — a revolutionary treatment that uses your body’s own healing power to restore hair health naturally and effectively.

What is GFC Therapy

GFC stands for *Growth Factor Concentrate, a highly concentrated plasma-derived solution containing powerful growth factors. These growth factors are extracted from your own blood and help in **repairing, regenerating, and stimulating hair follicles*, making it a cutting-edge solution for hair fall and thinning

How GFC is Made from Your Blood

  • 1. A small amount of your blood is collected.
  • 2. Using specially designed *GFC tubes* (like Dermaloops GFC), the blood is processed in a centrifuge.
  • 3. The result: a growth factor-rich plasma without red blood cells or white cells.
  • 4. This GFC solution is then *injected into the scalp* to stimulate dormant hair follicles and enhance growth.
  • 5. Unlike traditional PRP, GFC is *purer, more stable, and requires fewer sessions* for visible results.

Key Benefits of GFC Therapy

  • Clinically Proven: Backed by research and dermatologists
  • Safe & Natural: Autologous therapy = no chemicals, no allergy risk
  • Faster Results: Noticeable changes in just 2–3 sessions for many patients
  • No Downtime: Resume normal activities after treatment
  • Minimally Invasive: Simple injections, no surgery

Who is GFC Therapy Best For?

  • Men & women with early to moderate hair thinning
  • Patients looking for natural alternatives to hair transplant
  • Those who have already tried PRP but want better results
  • People with alopecia areata or patchy hair loss

What to Expect in a GFC Session

Each session lasts about *30–45 minutes*, including blood collection, processing, and injection.Results begin to show after 2–3 sessions, with fuller, thicker hair typically visible in 6–8 weeks.

"Aftercare Tips "

  • Avoid washing your hair for 12–24 hours post-session
  • Don’t use harsh shampoos or oils immediately
  • Follow your dermatologist’s instructions strictly
  • Combine with good nutrition & scalp care for best results

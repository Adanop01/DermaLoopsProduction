Platelet-Rich Plasma Tube

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy is a safe, non-surgical procedure that uses your own blood to promote healing, stimulate hair regrowth, and improve skin texture. It has become one of the most trusted treatments in aesthetic and regenerative medicine, offering natural results without the use of synthetic additives. Our PRP tube is prefilled with Gel and Sodium Citrate, enabling effective blood separation and optimal plasma yield. It ensures that red blood cells are filtered out while preserving platelets in a high-quality plasma layer. Widely used by dermatologists and trichologists, this tube is ideal for hair fall treatments, under-eye concerns, acne scars, and general skin rejuvenation. The procedure is minimally invasive, with no risk of allergic reaction, as it uses your own biology to repair and regenerate.

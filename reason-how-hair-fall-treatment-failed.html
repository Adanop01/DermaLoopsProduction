Common Reasons Hair Fall Treatments Fail — And How to Fix Them

There are several reasons why hair fall treatments may not work as expected. Here are some common mistakes and how to fix them:

1. No Proper Diagnosis

Jumping into treatments without knowing the cause (thyroid? stress? hormonal imbalance?) is like treating symptoms, not the disease.

✔ Fix: Always consult a dermatologist and get a scalp analysis or blood test before treatment.

2. Skipping Sessions

One session of PRP or GFC won’t show miracles. Hair growth is a gradual biological process.

✔ Fix: Stick to the prescribed number of sessions and follow through completely.

3. Poor Aftercare

Washing hair too early, applying oils or heat soon after treatment can damage progress.

✔ Fix: Follow a clean, gentle scalp routine and your doctor’s aftercare guide strictly.

4. Stress and Lifestyle

Even the best treatments won’t work if you’re not sleeping, eating well, or managing stress.

✔ Fix: Focus on whole-body wellness — diet, hydration, and mindfulness.

5. Low-Quality Products

Cheap, non-sterile tubes or untrained providers can ruin your results.

✔ Fix: Use CE-certified products like Dermaloops GFC/PRP tubes in a licensed clinic.
